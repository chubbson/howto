##### [[Cheatsheet]]

open emacs on console `emacs -Q -nw ./nodes.org`

open file: `c-x c-f`
safe file: `c-x c-s`

##### moving buffer:

Arrow keys: `left top down right`
next line: `c-n`
prev line: `c-p`
backwart char: `c-b`
exit emacs: `c-x c-c`

##### insert mode:

default mode

undo: `c-/`
redo: `c-x u`
